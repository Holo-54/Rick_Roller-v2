**Commands:**
/roll <# of sides>  -  Rolls a dice with the specified number of sides 
/multi_roll <# of sides> <# of dice> -  Rolls multiple of the same dice specified 

**Examples:** 
/roll 20  -  Rolls a d20 
/mutli_roll 6 4  -  Rolls 4 d6

Each command will have prompts built in to make things easier

*Note: Rick will only roll valid DND dice (100, 20, 12, 10, 8, 6, and 4 sided die)
